// faq

Questions buyers actually ask.

Straight answers, so the intro call can go straight to your situation.

What does fractional DevRel cost?

Every engagement is scoped to your goals, team, and timeline on the first call, and you get a written quote before any work starts. Retainers are sized so a senior fractional program costs meaningfully less than building the equivalent function in-house — and the number never changes without your written sign-off.

How fast can an engagement start?

Strategy sprints typically kick off within two weeks of a signed scope. Embedded retainers start as soon as the kickoff practitioner is matched — usually two to four weeks.

Does this replace hiring an in-house team?

For a while, deliberately. When you're ready to hire internally, we design the org, write the roles, and hand over the program. Success is you outgrowing us — it's commitment 06.

Who actually does the work?

The person in your kickoff is the person doing your work — a senior practitioner who has held the role in-house, with principal oversight on every engagement. No handoff to a junior bench after the contract is signed. Nobody sells you seniority and delivers juniority.

Can you work with enterprise procurement?

Yes. We contract through an established LLC, support MSAs and security review, and can invoice on your procurement cycle. Bring your legal team; we've met them before.

What's the minimum commitment?

RFC is a one-time 4–6 week engagement. Beta carries a 3-month minimum. GA carries a 6-month minimum. Office Hours runs month to month. Mainstage is priced per event.

We're not ready for a retainer. Where do we start?

Two doors: the RFC Strategy Sprint — a fixed-scope audit and roadmap — or Office Hours advisory. Both are built to earn the retainer, not assume it.

Can you run just our event?

Yes. Mainstage is available à la carte — full production, with sponsor-aligned challenge design and a post-event conversion playbook.
